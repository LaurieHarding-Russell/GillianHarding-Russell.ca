gillian harding-russell

gillian harding-russell was born in Toronto and grew up in St Jean, Quebec, outside of Montreal. She now lives in Regina, Saskatchewan.

She received her BA (Hons, 1st Class) and MA from McGill, and her Ph.D at the University of Saskatchewan. Her dissertation was on post-modern Canadian poetry: Open Form of Mythopoeia in Postmodern Canadian Poetry, including Michael Ondaatje and Gwendolyn MacEwen.
